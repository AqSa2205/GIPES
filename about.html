About Us

Welcome to Galaxy Pharma, where our commitment to healthcare excellence knows no bounds!


Establishment

Since our establishment in 2003, Galaxy Pharma has continuously evolved to become one of Pakistan's leading pharmaceutical companies. We take immense pride in our expertise across various fields, including Infertility, Gynecology, Oncology, Surgical/Orthopedic, Cosmetics/Aesthetics, and Diabetes/Anesthesia. As part of our growth, we have expanded our portfolio to include Nutraceuticals manufacturing, catering to the diverse needs of our customers. At Galaxy Pharma, we stand at the forefront of Women's Healthcare, offering an unparalleled range of products and services. Our journey began with a focused endeavor in infertility treatments, and from there, we have grown exponentially to encompass Oncology, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Nutraceuticals, Diagnostics, and Surgical Management. With a relentless pursuit of excellence, we have transitioned from a mere marketing firm to a state-of-the-art manufacturing facility located in the thriving Korangi Creek Industrial Park.

Our Commitment to Accessibility, Affordability, and Social Responsibility

Our dedication has earned us exclusive marketing and distribution rights in Pakistan from several renowned multinational pharmaceutical companies. By offering quality products that are bioequivalent to the industry leaders, we ensure accessibility and affordability for all members of our diverse community. At Galaxy Pharma, we prioritize the dissemination of scientific information and employ a dynamic marketing strategy that includes public awareness campaigns, disease prevention initiatives, scientific promotion, and effective customer relationship management. We consider social responsibility and the enhancement of the medical community as integral components of our philosophy. By continuously adding value to the lives of our patients and healthcare professionals, we strive to make a positive impact on society as a whole.
At Galaxy Pharma, our vision is to achieve unrivaled prominence in the healthcare sector. With a focus on innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we are determined to surpass expectations and establish new benchmarks within the industry. Join us on this transformative journey towards a healthier future. Experience the unrivaled excellence of Galaxy Pharma, where affordability meets uncompromising quality, and compassion meets innovation. For comprehensive information about our products, services, and initiatives, we invite you to explore our website or reach out to our dedicated team. Together, let us shape a healthier and brighter tomorrow.
